Add cross-approval check for role escalations on IT.AC.1.C01

opened 2026-09-15 14:08 UTC · 3 commits · 3 of 3 required approvals
What this changes. Adds a new substantive sufficiency rule to control IT.AC.1.C01: every role escalation detected in the access-review snapshot must have a matching approval ticket from the ServiceNow CR queue (type ITGC-CR). On failure, the evidence is marked inadequate at submission time with a specific diagnostic.

Why. Q3 override registry shows 3 false-pass disagreements with the same pattern: ECM was structurally adequate, reviewer found a substantive gap (the r.taylor escalation). Adding this rule should lift substantive agreement on this control from ~74% back toward the 85% threshold.

Scope. This rule is scoped to IT.AC.1.C01 specifically — it does not affect any other access-review control. The universal layer (structural checks) is unchanged.
