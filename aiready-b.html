Evidence schema · IT.AC.1.C01

v2.3 · last updated 2026-09-10 by Sarah Park

Control summary

IT.AC.1.C01
Quarterly user access review on the production database cluster. The control operates when a designated reviewer has, in the last quarter, examined the active account roster, attested to its accuracy, and surfaced exceptions for remediation.

Required evidence shape

access_roster file/csv|pdf required
An export of all accounts active on prod-db-01 during the cycle. Must contain columns: user, role, last_login, manager_attested.
cycle_period date_range required
Coverage period — must end within 5 business days of cycle close (2026-09-30).
dba_attestation file/eml|pdf required
Signed attestation from active DBA personnel. Signer must be employed at the time of signature.
reviewer_signoff signature required
Internal Audit reviewer's verdict + Pencil annotations (collected at review time, not submission).
approval_ticket_refs cross-link[] required
For every role escalation in access_roster, a matching approval ticket ID from ServiceNow CR queue. Empty if no escalations.
carryover_findings finding_ref[] Optional
References to prior-quarter findings still open in HEL queue. Pre-populated from last sign-off.

State 1. The schema is the contract between the control and the agent. Schema lives next to the control definition, owned by the PMO, versioned in the policy-as-code repo. When the schema changes, every owner whose evidence depends on it gets a structured diff — not a memo. Click "Next" to see Weston submit against this schema.
