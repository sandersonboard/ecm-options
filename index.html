1 — The main reframe

It's two products, not one

What we've been calling "one product with many UX options" is actually two products with different strategic logic. The portfolio splits cleanly along this line.

Reminder agent

The press-release win

Meets owners where they work. Distribution play; the moat is weak and copyable.

Primary user: Control owner
Moat character: Distribution & behavioral lock-in
Sufficiency agent

What builds the company

Judges whether evidence actually proves the control operated. The structural moat — substantive rules tied to specific controls, signed off by the audit committee — compounds.

Primary user: Reviewer / external auditor
Moat character: Structural rules per control

3 — The honest caveat

The prototypes prove the design is plausible — not that the strategy is right

Three load-bearing assumptions are still untested. The prototypes test design intent. The strategy gets tested by data.
