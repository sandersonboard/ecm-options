Three of the four substantive overrides this quarter shared a pattern: ECM was structurally adequate but missed cross-system substantive sufficiency (escalations without approval tickets, departed attesters, ServiceNow workflow comments treated as inferior to email). The structural agreement line is steady at 96% — ECM's foundation is solid.

This is the signal that the substantive layer needs new rules. The override registry has the training data, the model card has the change-process, the policy-as-code repo has the place to write the new checks.
