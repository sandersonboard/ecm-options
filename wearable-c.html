Calibration

The footer "Avg 0.9/day" is the trust metric. If alerts per day climb above ~2, the watch loses its meaning. Below that, it earns the right to demand attention.

Trade-off

Calibration is the hard part — set the threshold too low and the watch becomes noise; set it too high and Weston misses something that matters. The point is to stay silent until silence would actually be wrong.
